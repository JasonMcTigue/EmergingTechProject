Galway-Mayo Institute Of Technology

GMIT has four libraries in different campus locations - the Galway Campus, Centre for Creative Arts and Media (CCAM), Mayo Campus, and Letterfrack Campus. Our library stock is fully automated and all our print materials are classified according to the Dewey Decimal Classification system..

Our Mission

The mission of the library is to promote a learning culture within GMIT by acquiring, organising, disseminating and providing access to learning resources and services in a congenial environment. The library will support teaching, learning, research and regional development by providing a customer based service to our students, staff and stakeholders.
