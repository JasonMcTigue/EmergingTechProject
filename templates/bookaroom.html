Conditions of Use

A maximum of 8 students are permitted in each group study room. Students are required to keep noise levels down. Students must vacate rooms promptly at the end of a study session and remove all belongings. No eating or drinking is permitetd in the group study rooms (water is fine).Rooms that remain unoccupied 15 minutes after the allotted start time are deemed vacant and will be made available to other groups.

How to book a room

Students can book a room using the computers located by the front desk of the library. Students should book these rooms in advance to ensure they get the study room. STudents are reminded to confirm their booking through their student email.
